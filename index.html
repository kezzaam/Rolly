Rolly

The rolly neat dice generator

How to use
  • Select the number of faces for your dice. This can be as simple as a coin toss (2 faces) or as complex as 144.
  • Select the number of dice you'd like to roll, from 1 - 10.
  • Click the button to roll the dice and see your results.
